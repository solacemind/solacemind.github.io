Your daily dose of empathy. Free, private, and stigma-free.

SolaceMind is a groundbreaking, free AI platform designed to democratize access to mental health resources. Our core offering is a structured, private support session with an empathetic AI listener.

We aim to dismantle common barriers to traditional therapy including cost, social stigma, and lack of awarenessby providing a private, judgment free space for processing difficult thoughts and challenges.
