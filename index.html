SolaceMind

Accessible AI for Emotional Support

Your daily dose of empathy. Free, private, and stigma-free.

SolaceMind is a groundbreaking, free AI platform designed to democratize access to mental health resources. Our core offering is a structured, private support session with an empathetic AI listener.

We aim to dismantle common barriers to traditional therapy—including cost, social stigma, and lack of awareness—by providing a private, judgment-free space for processing difficult thoughts and challenges.

*Currently in the Concept and Validation phase. Follow our repository for updates.*
